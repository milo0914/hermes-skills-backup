# Suno AI Lyrics Template

Quick reference template for Suno AI lyrics formatting.

## Required Format

```
## Song Title

Style:
<genre, sub-genre, vocal style, etc.> (Max 120 characters, comma-separated in English)

```lyrics
[Intro]
[Sound effect]

[Verse 1]
Verse text

[Chorus]
Chorus text

[Verse 2]
Verse text

[Outro]
Outro text

[END]
```

## Song Section Tags

- `[Intro]` - Opening section
- `[Verse 1]`, `[Verse 2]`, etc. - Story sections
- `[Chorus]` - Emotional core, main hook
- `[Rap Verse]` - Rap-style verse
- `[(Style) Verse/Chorus]` - Indicate style in parentheses (e.g., `[Sad Verse]`, `[Power Chorus]`)
- `[Bridge]` - Detour or shift in perspective
- `[Outro]` - Farewell section
- `[Instrumental intro/outro]` - Instrumental sections

## Sound Effect Tags

- `[Laughter]`
- `[Cry]`
- `[Scream]`
- `[Whisper: "TEXT"]` - Must be in same language as lyrics
- `[Sound of typing]`
- `[Gunshot]`
- `[Slowed and Reverbed Sample: "TEXT"]`
- `[Censored beep]`

*Almost anything can be written in brackets as a sound effect.*

## Music Style Examples

```
abstract hip hop, lo-fi
cinematic orchestral spy thriller, 1960s Cold War era, smoky sultry female vocalist
90s grunge, raw vocals, heavy distortion
```

**Rules:**
- Max 120 characters
- Comma-separated
- English only
- No artist names or trademarks
